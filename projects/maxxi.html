MAXXI&BVLGARI Prize

Document and celebrate the emerging wave of next-generation artists

Maxxi&Bulgari Prize is an award that recognizes young artists on an international scale. In collaboration with Leftloft, we worked on developing a compelling campaign and catalogue for the 2018 event and exhibition. In the light of integrating Bulgari's brand character, we used unconventional applications of gold palettes, our objective was to fuse the inherent values of a luxury brand with the dynamic realm of contemporary art. This integration aimed to captivate audiences and celebrate the boundless creativity of emerging talents.

This transformative journey came with the launch of the new identity, celebrated through a captivating campaign centred around the iconic American photographer, Robert Mapplethorpe. Through compelling visuals, and engaging narratives, the campaign served as a captivating introduction to Madre's renewed persona, capturing the contemporary art lovers worldwide.

We took the challenge of redesigning Madre's corporate image, infusing it with a fresh sense of purpose and relevance. The visual identity was thoughtfully reimagined, paying homage to the museum's rich heritage and Naples' local cultural dynamics.
